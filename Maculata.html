Begonia Maculata

Begonia maculata is a cane-like species native to Brazil. On this selection the large, long leaves are olive green on the top covered with large silver dots. The back of the leaf is red. It has large clusters of white flowers.

Type: cane

Watering: keep soil moist but allow top two inches to dry between watering

Light: indirect sunlight or high flourescent light

humidity: moderate to high
