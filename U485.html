Begonia U485

Listed in the ABS unidentified species list, there is no information with entry. Species with high iridescent red leaves with blue highlights. Very red under leaves and stems. Top of leaves covered in red hairs and often has tiny pinkish white spotting on tops of leaves. For terrarium/greenhouse culture only.

Type: Rex

Watering: keep soil moist

Light: indirect sunlight or high flourescent light

humidity: moderate to high
