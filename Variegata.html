Begonia Hemsleyana Variegata

Begonia hemsleyana variegata (formerly known as U404) is a form of species from China, hemsleyana. The compound leaves are dark green and covered with lots of silver spots. When first introduced it was given the unidentified species number U404 by the American Begonia Society. It has now been identified as a form of hemsleyana. It is a rhizomatous begonia jointed at the soil surface, with upright stems. This makes a really exotic specimen plant. Give it a little extra humidity and keep from extreme heat. It does very well in a large terrarium or vivarium.

Type: cane

Watering: keep soil moist but allow top two inches to dry between watering.

Light: indirect sunlight or high flourescent light

humidity: moderate to high
