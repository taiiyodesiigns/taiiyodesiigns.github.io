Begonia Looking Glass

Begonia ‘Looking Glass’ is a cane-like hybrid which has beautiful silver leaves with wide olive green veins The back of the leaf is red. It is a fast and easy to grow upright begonia, although if not staked it will fall over the sides of a container. It has medium sized clusters of pink flowers. Give it bright light and allow it to get slightly dry between watering.

Type: cane

Watering: keep soil moist but allow top two inches to dry between watering

Light: indirect sunlight or high flourescent light

humidity: low to high
